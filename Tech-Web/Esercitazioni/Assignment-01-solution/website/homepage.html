The Book of Programming

This website contains fragments from the legendary Book of Programming, an ancient tome lost to time.

The book is said to have been written by a disciple of Fu-Tzu, the legendary master programmer.

Currently, two sections have been recovered:

  • a section describing the two aspects of programming (data and control);
  • a section reporting short-sayings and other wisdom of the master Fu-Tzu.
